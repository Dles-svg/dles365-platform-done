## How Rentals Work

### Billing Structure
- **1 Hour Minimum:** All rentals start with a 1-hour minimum
- **Hourly Intervals:** Rentals are billed in 1-hour intervals
- **Daily Billing:** Charges accumulate and are billed daily
- **Auto-Renewal:** Equipment stays rented until user ends the rental

### For Miners (Equipment Owners):
1. **List Equipment:** Register hardware and select the appropriate tier
2. **Set Availability:** Control when equipment is available for rent
3. **Automatic Billing:** Users are charged hourly in DL365 tokens
4. **Instant Payouts:** Receive DL365 tokens directly to your wallet every hour
5. **Rating System:** Build reputation through successful rentals

### For Gamers/Users (Renters):
1. **Browse Equipment:** Filter by type (gaming/mining/computing) and tier
2. **Check Specs:** Review detailed specifications before renting
3. **Rent in 1-Hour Blocks:** Pay per hour used with DL365 tokens
4. **Remote Access:** Get instant connection details to access equipment
5. **End Anytime:** Stop rental when finished to avoid further charges
6. **Rate & Review:** Help others choose quality equipment
